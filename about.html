We are located at Tom Mboya streets in Nairobi. Ava shoes was established in 2024. You can explore some stylish shoes in our shop.

We’ve designed a shoe for all: Nike Motiva. Women-led insights have helped us innovate and create a shoe that can make movement feel easier and more fun and leave people wanting to come back for more.

Without New England's fierce and demanding environs, there would be no Ava shoes. We started by equipping workers to power through the toughest outdoor environments.

Ever since the day we created our first yellow boot, we’ve been inspired by that gritty New England tendency to ‘think outside the box’ and create innovative solutions to everyday problems.
